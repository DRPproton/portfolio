Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I offer a range of data science and analytics services including machine learning model development, data analysis, data visualization, and custom dashboard creation. I can also help with data strategy and consulting.

Do you work remotely?

Yes, I work remotely with clients from all over the world. I'm comfortable with different time zones and can adapt to your team's schedule for meetings and collaboration.

What is your typical project process?

My process typically involves an initial consultation to understand your needs, followed by data exploration and analysis, model development, testing, and deployment. I maintain clear communication throughout and provide regular updates on progress.

I take data privacy and security very seriously. I follow industry best practices for data protection, use secure channels for data transfer, and can sign NDAs as needed. I'm also familiar with GDPR and other data protection regulations.
